History

In an ever-evolving world where technology connects us like never before, RS Tuff Enterprises began its journey in 2013 as Kanha Glass, a small retail outlet specializing in glass trading. As the demand for glass in the Indian market surged, we rebranded to Rakesh Glass in 2017 to better reflect our expanding vision.

In the years that followed, Rakesh Glass grew significantly, adding two more firms to create a larger and more diverse outlet. Now operating as RS Tuff Enterprises, we have established branches across several districts, including RS Tuff in Bhopal, RS Tuff Glass in Indore, and RS Tuff in Jabalpur.

Looking ahead, our goal is to broaden our presence by opening additional branches throughout India, reinforcing our commitment to being a leading provider of comprehensive glass solutions.
